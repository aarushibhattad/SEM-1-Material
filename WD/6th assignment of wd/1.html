IEEE IMS

Introduction:

IEEE IMS (Instrumentation & Measurement Society) is a technical club dedicated to advancing the field of instrumentation, measurement, and related technologies. Our club fosters learning, innovation, and professional growth by providing a platform for students to explore, research, and apply measurement techniques.

Mission:

  • Organize technical sessions, workshops, and competitions
  • Provide exposure to industry trends and innovations
  • Foster research and development culture
  • Collaborate with IEEE chapters and industry professionals

Objective:

Bridge the gap between theoretical knowledge and practical applications through hands-on workshops, seminars, and collaborative projects.
