Team Quark

Introduction:

Team Quark is a dynamic technical club driven by the motto “Ideate, Innovate, Collaborate.” Our mission is to nurture emerging and underrepresented technologies while fostering a passionate community of students.

Mission:

To foster technical innovation, skill development, and collaborative problem-solving by participating in hackathons and exploring emerging domains such as Rust and ROS.

Objective:

To establish Quark as a leading technical club that pioneers solutions in least-explored technical domains and empowers students globally.
